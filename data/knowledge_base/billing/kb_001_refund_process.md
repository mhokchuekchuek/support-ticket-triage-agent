## Overview

To request a refund, navigate to **Settings > Billing > Request Refund**. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval.

## Eligibility

- **Monthly plans**: Full refund available within 7 days of charge
- **Annual plans**: Pro-rated refund available within 30 days of purchase
- **Enterprise plans**: Contact your account manager for custom refund terms

## How to Request

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Settings** in the top-right menu
3. Click **Billing**
4. Select **Request Refund**
5. Choose the charge you want refunded
6. Provide a reason for the refund request
7. Click **Submit Request**

## Processing Time

- Approval decision: 1-2 business days
- Refund processing: 5-7 business days after approval
- Bank processing: May take additional 3-5 days depending on your bank

## Important Notes

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Partial refunds are available for unused portions of annual plans
- Usage-based charges (API calls, storage overage) are non-refundable
- If you received promotional credits, those are forfeited upon refund

## Need Help?

If your refund request is urgent or you have questions, contact our billing team at billing@example.com or use the in-app chat support.
