## Accessing Invoices

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Billing History**
2. Click on any transaction to view the invoice
3. Use **Download PDF** for a printable version
4. Use **Email Invoice** to send to another address

## Invoice Components

### Header Information
- Invoice number and date
- Your billing address
- Our company information

### Line Items
- **Base subscription**: Your plan's monthly/annual fee
- **Additional users**: Per-user charges beyond plan limits
- **Storage overage**: Charges for exceeding storage quota
- **API usage**: Charges for API calls beyond included quota
- **Add-ons**: Premium features or integrations

### Taxes
- Sales tax (US customers in applicable states)
- VAT (EU customers)
- GST (Australian customers)
- Tax amount varies by location

## Billing Cycle

- **Monthly plans**: Billed on the same date each month
- **Annual plans**: Billed once per year on signup date
- **Prorated charges**: When upgrading mid-cycle

## Tax-Exempt Status

If your organization is tax-exempt:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Tax Information**
2. Upload your tax exemption certificate
3. Enter your tax ID/EIN
4. Allow 2-3 business days for verification
5. Future invoices will exclude applicable taxes

## Updating Billing Address

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Billing Information**
2. Click **Edit** next to billing address
3. Update your address details
4. Changes apply to future invoices only

## Invoice for Reimbursement

Need an invoice for expense reporting?
- All invoices include company name and address
- Add a PO number in **Settings > Billing > PO Number**
- Download PDF format for expense submissions

## Historical Invoices

- All invoices are available for the lifetime of your account
- Filter by date range or amount
- Bulk download available for annual summaries
