## Supported Payment Methods

- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **PayPal**: Available for all plan types
- **Wire Transfer**: Enterprise plans only (minimum $1,000)
- **ACH Bank Transfer**: US customers on annual plans

## Updating Your Card

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add New Card** or **Update** on existing card
3. Enter your new card details
4. Click **Save**
5. Optionally set as default payment method

## Adding PayPal

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add PayPal**
3. You'll be redirected to PayPal to authorize
4. Complete authorization and return to our site
5. PayPal will appear as a payment option

## Wire Transfer (Enterprise)

For wire transfers:
1. Contact your account manager or email enterprise@example.com
2. Request wire transfer instructions
3. Include your account ID in the transfer reference
4. Allow 3-5 business days for processing

## Handling Expired Cards

If your card expires:
- You'll receive email reminders 30, 14, and 7 days before expiration
- Update your card before the next billing cycle to avoid service interruption
- If payment fails, you have a 7-day grace period to update

## Failed Payment Recovery

If a payment fails:
1. We retry automatically after 24 hours
2. You'll receive an email notification
3. Update your payment method within 7 days
4. Service continues during the grace period
5. After 7 days, your account may be downgraded to Free

## Security

- All payment data is encrypted (PCI-DSS compliant)
- We never store full card numbers
- You can remove saved payment methods at any time
