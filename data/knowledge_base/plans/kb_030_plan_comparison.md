## Plan Overview

We offer three plans to fit different needs:

| Feature | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|------|-----|------------|
| **Price** | $0/month | $15/user/month | Custom pricing |
| **Users** | Up to 5 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Storage** | 1 GB | 50 GB | Unlimited |
| **Projects** | 3 | Unlimited | Unlimited |

## Free Plan

Perfect for individuals and small teams getting started.

### Included
- Up to 5 team members
- 3 active projects
- 1 GB storage
- Basic integrations (Slack, GitHub)
- Community support
- Mobile apps

### Limitations
- Limited export formats (CSV only)
- No API access
- No advanced permissions
- No audit logs
- 7-day activity history

## Pro Plan

For growing teams that need more power and flexibility.

### Included (everything in Free, plus)
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited projects
- 50 GB storage per workspace
- All integrations
- Priority email support
- API access
- Advanced permissions and roles
- Custom fields
- 1-year activity history
- Dark mode and themes
- Advanced export formats (JSON, XLSX)

### Pricing
- **Monthly**: $15/user/month
- **Annual**: $12/user/month (20% savings)

## Enterprise Plan

For organizations with advanced security, compliance, and support needs.

### Included (everything in Pro, plus)
- Unlimited storage
- SSO/SAML integration
- SCIM provisioning
- Custom data retention
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 phone support
- SLA guarantees (99.9% uptime)
- Advanced audit logs
- Data residency options
- Custom contracts
- Training and onboarding
- Unlimited activity history

### Pricing
- Custom pricing based on needs
- Contact sales@example.com

## Choosing the Right Plan

- **Free**: Testing the product, personal use, small teams
- **Pro**: Growing teams, need integrations and API access
- **Enterprise**: Large organizations, compliance requirements, custom needs
