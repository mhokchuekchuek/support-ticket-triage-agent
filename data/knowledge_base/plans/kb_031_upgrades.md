## How to Upgrade

### From Free to Pro

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Plans**
2. Click **Upgrade to Pro**
3. Choose billing cycle (monthly/annual)
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm upgrade
6. Access new features immediately

### From Pro to Enterprise

1. Contact our sales team:
   - Email: sales@example.com
   - Or click "Contact Sales" in Settings > Billing
2. Discuss your requirements
3. Receive custom quote
4. Sign enterprise agreement
5. Onboarding with dedicated team

## Billing for Upgrades

### Mid-Cycle Upgrades (Free → Pro)

When upgrading mid-billing cycle:
- You're charged immediately for the new plan
- Previous paid amount (if any) is prorated and credited
- Example: Upgrading on day 15 of 30-day cycle = 50% credit

### Prorated Calculation

```
Days remaining in cycle: 15
Monthly Pro cost: $15/user
Prorated charge: $15 × (15/30) = $7.50/user
```

### Annual Plan Upgrades

- Upgrading from monthly to annual: Immediate switch with prorated credit
- 20% discount on annual billing
- Can pay remaining annual amount upfront or continue monthly until cycle ends

## Adding Users

When you add users on Pro plan:
- New users billed immediately (prorated)
- Added to next billing cycle automatically
- Bulk user discounts available for 50+ users

## Downgrading

### Pro to Free
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select Free plan
4. Downgrade takes effect at end of current billing cycle
5. Export data before downgrade (some features restricted)

### What Happens on Downgrade
- Extra users beyond Free limit will lose access
- Projects beyond limit become read-only
- Pro features become unavailable
- Data is NOT deleted (just access-restricted)

### Reactivation
- Upgrade again anytime to restore full access
- All data preserved during downgrade period
- No setup required—features re-enable instantly
