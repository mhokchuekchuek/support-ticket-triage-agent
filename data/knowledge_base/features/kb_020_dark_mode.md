## Current Status

Dark mode is currently **available on Pro and Enterprise plans**. Free plan users can access dark mode as part of our public beta.

## Enabling Dark Mode

### Web Application

1. Click your profile avatar (top-right)
2. Select **Settings**
3. Go to **Appearance**
4. Choose from:
   - **Light**: Always use light theme
   - **Dark**: Always use dark theme
   - **System**: Match your OS preference

### Desktop App

1. Open **Preferences** (Cmd/Ctrl + ,)
2. Select **Appearance** tab
3. Choose your preferred theme

1. Tap the menu icon (â‰¡)
2. Go to **Settings > Display**
3. Select theme preference
4. Or enable "Use System Setting" to match device theme

## Browser Extension

The browser extension follows your main app settings. Changes sync automatically.

## Known Limitations

- Some embedded content may not respect dark mode
- PDF previews display in original colors
- Third-party integrations may show in light mode
- Email notifications are always light themed

## Accessibility Considerations

- Dark mode reduces eye strain in low-light conditions
- High contrast options available in Settings > Accessibility
- Custom color schemes available on Enterprise plans

## Roadmap

## Workarounds (Free Plan)

While waiting for full access:
- Use browser extensions like "Dark Reader"
- Enable system-wide dark mode on your OS
- Join our beta program for early access
