## Support Channels

### Help Center (All Plans)
- Self-service documentation
- Searchable knowledge base
- Available 24/7
- URL: help.example.com

### Email Support
- **Pro & Enterprise**: support@example.com
- Response time: Within 24 hours (Pro), 4 hours (Enterprise)
- Include account email in message

### Live Chat
- Available in-app (bottom-right widget)
- **Free**: Business hours only (9am-5pm PT, M-F)
- **Pro**: Extended hours (6am-10pm PT, M-F)
- **Enterprise**: 24/7 availability

### Phone Support (Enterprise Only)
- Dedicated support line
- 24/7 availability
- Number provided in your welcome packet
- Callback option available

## Business Hours

Standard support hours (non-Enterprise):
- Monday - Friday
- 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM Pacific Time
- Excluding US holidays

## Getting Faster Support

### Include This Information
1. Account email address
2. Workspace name (if applicable)
3. Browser/device information
4. Screenshot of the issue
5. Steps to reproduce the problem
6. Error messages (exact text)

### Priority Queue
- Enterprise customers receive priority
- Critical issues (data loss, security) escalated automatically
- Use "Urgent" flag in subject for critical issues

## Support Categories

When contacting support, select the appropriate category:

- **Billing**: Charges, refunds, plan changes
- **Technical**: Bugs, errors, performance issues
- **Account**: Login, settings, security
- **Feature Request**: Suggestions for improvements
- **Security**: Vulnerabilities, suspicious activity

## What We Can Help With

### We Can Assist With
- Account and billing issues
- Technical troubleshooting
- Feature questions
- Integration setup
- Data recovery requests
- Security concerns

### We Cannot Help With
- Third-party integration internal issues
- Custom development work
- Training (see Enterprise offerings)
- Legal questions (contact legal@example.com)

## Escalation Process

If your issue isn't resolved:

1. **Reply to existing ticket** - Don't create new one
2. **Request escalation** - Ask for supervisor review
3. **Enterprise**: Contact your account manager directly
4. **Severe issues**: Email escalations@example.com
